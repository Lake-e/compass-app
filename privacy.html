Compass App Privacy

Here's how we handle user data:

Location data: We only send approximate location data (to about the nearest mile) to Texas A&M GeoServices for determining the nearest city name to display. According to Texas A&M, they do not store or track user data. You can read more about their privacy policy here. Precise location data says on your device.

Cookies: The app may use cookies for functional purposes. Google Analytics is use to gauge how many users are using our app, and may also use cookies. We do not collect any personally identifiable information, but we have no control over how Google uses cookies. For more information, please see Google's privacy policy.

The app does not track your activities to target advertizements or use your data for any other purpose.
